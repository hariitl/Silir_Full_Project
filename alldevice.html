    About

    Silir is a pioneering AI-powered IoT system that brings automation, precision, and intelligence to mushroom farming. Developed by iYarKai Tech Lab, we have successfully implemented our smart farming solutions across 36 districts in Tamil Nadu, empowering over 2000+ women self-help groups and enabling sustainable One Village – One Mushroom Unit models.

    Revolutionizing Mushroom Farming with AI & IoT

    • NASSCOM AI GameChanger Award – AgriTech Segment
    • 🌍 World Bank-Funded Initiative for Rural Women Entrepreneurship
    • 💡 Optimized Cultivation Process Backed by AI & Data Science

    At Silir, we’re building the future of agriculture, ensuring higher yields, reduced resource wastage, and sustainable farming practices.
